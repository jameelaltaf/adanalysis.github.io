Three critical problems
Problem 1 — Under 30% close rate is your biggest revenue leak
You generated 3,121 conversations. At <30% close, you booked ~900 sessions. At $250 avg = ~$225K potential revenue. You captured $120K — meaning ~$105K walked away in unconverted WhatsApp chats. Fixing your close rate is worth more than doubling your ad budget. At 50% close with the same leads, you'd generate $195K — a $75K increase with zero extra spend.
Problem 2 — Frequency 7.79 means your audience is exhausted
For a local Mississauga/GTA studio, your addressable audience of pregnant women and new mothers is naturally limited (~80,000–120,000 people at any time). Showing the same ad 7–8 times explains your rising CPR. You need fresh creatives rotating every 3 weeks and audience expansion to surrounding cities.
Problem 3 — Sub-$300 pricing limits your ROAS ceiling
At $250 average package, a 40% price increase to $350 takes your ROAS from 6× to 8.4× instantly — with zero extra ad spend. Premium pricing also attracts more committed clients, reducing low-intent leads in your DMs.
ROAS sensitivity — what moves the needle most
Objective recommendation: Click-to-WhatsApp (Leads objective)
Since you close 100% through WhatsApp manually, Click-to-WhatsApp is purpose-built for you. People tap the ad → WhatsApp opens instantly → you're already in conversation. No form, no redirect, no friction.

3-creative rotation plan
Run 3 ads per ad set — swap the weakest every 3 weeks
Creative A — Static Collage (always on): Your proven winner. Keep one version always active. Rotate a new angle every 3 weeks: before/after poses, behind-the-scenes setup, parent reaction moments, seasonal themes.

Creative B — Evergreen Carousel: Take the Christmas carousel (your 2.35% CTR winner), strip seasonal elements, rebuild as an evergreen portfolio carousel with 5–6 slides of your best shots.

Creative C — Video (test): 15-second reel. First 3 seconds = beautiful newborn photo + text "Mississauga families trust us with their first moments." Middle = 5-second montage. End = "Message us to check availability." No voiceover needed.

Three levers to double your ROAS — ranked by impact
Lever 1 — Fix WhatsApp close rate: 30% → 50% (highest impact, zero extra spend)
3,121 leads × 30% × $250 = $234K (current). Same leads × 50% × $250 = $390K. That's a $156K increase with zero extra ad spend. ROAS jumps from 6× to 10×.
Lever 2 — Introduce a premium package at $450–600
Even 20% of clients upgrading from $250 to $500 shifts your average order value meaningfully. List the premium package first in your WhatsApp pitch, then offer the base as the "more accessible option." ROAS scales with order value, not ad spend.
Lever 3 — Reduce CPR from $4.50 → $3.00 through smarter targeting
Shift budget to Lookalike audiences (proven $3.30 CPR), rotate creatives before frequency exceeds 3, switch to Click-to-WhatsApp objective, and add retargeting. A 33% CPR reduction means 33% more leads on the same budget.
